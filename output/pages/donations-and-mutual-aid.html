Donations and Mutual Aid

Wrecktangle and Smitten Kitten

Wrecktangle and Smitten Kitten are teaming up to get food and home essentials to families in need. You can buy a pizza at Wrecktangle and they will give a pizza to a family in need through Sunday 1/11.

Smitten Kitten is accepting non-perishable food donations. Drop off donations at the Lyn Lake Wrecktangle and Smitten Kitten location.
